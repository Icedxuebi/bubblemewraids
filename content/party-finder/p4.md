## Self-Marking for Spreads

If a mechanic needs you to spread, **self-mark** so partners are unambiguous:

- **Short spread** → mark yourself with **bind 1 🔗 / bind 2 🔗**. (Timer around 40-50 seconds)
- **Long spread** → mark yourself with **attack 1 ⦸ / attack 2 ⦸**. (Timer above 1 minute)

![Self-mark — short spread = attack 1/2, long spread = prohibited 1/2](/p4/self-mark.png)
