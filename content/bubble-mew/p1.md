## Graven Image 1

### Step 1 — Knockback to lineup + Fire/Ice rings

![Graven Image 1 — Fire/Ice rings](/p1/graven1.png)

A statue tethers to **all Supports** or **all DPS** and knockbacks everyone toward their lineup spot. While landing, Kefka simultaneously telegraphs two rings of orbs:

| Ring | Element | Default Mechanic |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| Top ring | Fire | Stack or Spread (telegraphed) |
| Bottom ring | Ice/Lightning | 90° Cones |

> **? Orb rule:** If an orb on the ring shows a **?**, it does the *opposite* mechanic.
> - ? Fire orb → reverses Stack/Spread
> - ? Ice orb → stand *inside* the AOE instead of dodging the cone

**Lineup — use the knockback to land here:**

| ← West (Supports) | East (DPS) → |
|---|---|
| H1 · H2 · MT · OT | D1 · D2 · D3 · D4 |

Resolve the Fire/Ice ring while landing from the knockback.

---

### Step 2 — Spread + Lasers + Towers

![Graven Image 1 — Laser spread](/p1/graven1-2.png)

After rings resolve, all 8 players spread out **West → East** across the arena.

Boss shoots lasers at **2 random Supports** and **2 random DPS**:
- Hit players receive a **Vuln** and a **tower spawns** beneath them.
- The 4 players **not** hit soak the towers in **left-to-right priority**:

> **H1 > MT > D1 > D4**

---

### Step 3 — Confetti Debuff knockback + Ice/Lightning

![Graven Image 1 — Towers and Confetti](/p1/graven1-3.png)

Boss applies **Confetti Debuff** to 1 Support and 1 DPS. On expiry it **knockbacks all nearby players** and passes to a random player. Use the knockback to dodge the next Ice/Lightning ring.

## Graven Image 2

![Graven Image 2 — Gravity puddles](/p1/graven2.png)

**Light Party composition:**
- **LP1** (North): MT · H1 · D1 · D3
- **LP2** (South): OT · H2 · D2 · D4

### Step 1 — First Gravity puddles (North)

Boss telegraphs a True or ? Ice AOE. Statue tethers all players in two sets:

| Tether | Type | Mechanic |
|--------|------|----------|
| Central Tether | Gravity | Stack AOE — stay together |
| Right Side Tether | Rock | Spread AOE that detonates Gravity Puddles |

**LP1 stacks North** on the marker to place the first Gravity puddle, dodging the Ice AOE. Spread out enough to see your own tethers.

When Gravity resolves, **Rock players spread away** from the Gravity stack:
- G1 (LP1): spread **left** facing boss
- G2 (LP2): spread **right** facing boss

Gravity players **move down the middle**.

---

### Step 2 — Ruin III Tankbuster + Half-room cleave

Boss casts **Ruin III Tankbuster** — tank swap, face buster **North**. Statue summons an orb for a **half-room cleave** — dodge to the safe half. OT uses invuln.

---

### Step 3 — Second Gravity puddles (South)

**LP2 moves South** to place the second set of Gravity puddles. Repeat the same spread pattern:
- G1: left facing boss · G2: right facing boss
- Gravity players down the middle

Boss does another **half-room cleave** — dodge to safe half.

---

### Step 4 — Confetti knockback through puddles

Boss applies **Confetti Debuff** to 1 Support and 1 DPS. Party stacks **inside the hitbox**:
- Confetti players move out to **max melee, next to puddles**
- **Supports** with Confetti → North · **DPS** with Confetti → South

Confetti expires → **knockback** drives the party through the Gravity Puddles to pop them for the puzzle. Confetti players move back in to help soak after.

---

> **Differs from PF?** See the [PF-standard strat](../party-finder/p1.md) for comparison.

## Graven Image 3 — Arrows (Tele-Portent)

### Step 1 — Place your teleporters

![Graven Image 3 — Arrow placement](/p1/betterarrows.png)

Boss casts **Tele-Portent**, giving each player **2 arrow debuffs**. When they expire they place a teleporter. You will have either:
- **2 matching arrows** — same debuff (①)
- **2 different arrows** — different debuffs (②)

---

### Step 2 — Tether resolution

![Graven Image 3 — Role positions](/p1/arrowgaze.png)

After teleporters are placed, statues tether to players:

| Tether colour | Resolve |
|---------------|---------|
| **Yellow** statue | Move **outside** (away from center) |
| **Purple** statue | Stay **middle** on your colour waymark |
