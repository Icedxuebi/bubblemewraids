## Boss Overview

**God Kefka.** This phase is the **Forsaken** sequence — a long chain of clone baits and **8 towers**
where the towers themselves anchor your sense of left/right for the *entire* phase. The hard part is
not knowing *where* your tower is; it's **eyes and spacing** inside it (cone vs. stack vs. spread)
and being fast enough on the clone baits that you don't mis-turn a clone.

We run the **Kroxy-Rinon 3/4/1 (Center / N stacks)** strat. This is the same resolution used in
Party Finder — see [the PF page](../party-finder/p2.md), which points at the same raidplan.

> **Source raidplan:** <https://raidplan.io/plan/UATE__aDcw1-bgVv> (read every page; the last page —
> circle-marker variant — is **not** used). Sim it: <https://github.com/WCGH/Waju-Sims/releases>.

## Waymarks

This strat **only works with the Diamond / box waymark preset** (12y):

| Mark | Spot | Mark | Spot |
|------|------|------|------|
| **A** | N (12y) | **1** | NW |
| **B** | E (12y) | **2** | NE |
| **C** | S (12y) | **3** | SE |
| **D** | W (12y) | **4** | SW |

- **Letter marks (A/B/C/D)** = the **front edge** of a tower (toward the boss).
- **Number marks (1/2/3/4)** = the **back edge** of a tower (away from the boss).

> The numbers in the raidplan slides are just illustrating position — *1 is not literally two spots
> over from A in game.* What matters is **where you stand on the marker next to your tower.**

## 1 — Clone Baiting

![Clone Baiting](/p2/page02.png)

Bait the clones **opposite of the towers**:

- **Supports → Left**
- **DPS → Right**

**Melee clone baiting = on the OUTER hitbox ring.** You bait the cone that is in the tower; the other
two are baited by the cones in the tower.

> ⚠️ **If you are on the INNER hitbox ring, you die.**

---

## 2 — Past / Future

![Past / Future](/p2/page03.png)

**Everyone baits**, stacked at **MAX MELEE**. Positions resolve as **Past**, **Future**, and
**"Far Future."**

> ⚠️ **If you are slow doing this, you cause mis-turned clones and wipe the party.** Get in and out
> fast.

## 3 — Forsaken: The 8 Towers

![Tower Resolutions — overview](/p2/page01.png)

The towers run **1 → 8** and **anchor your POV for left/right for all of Forsaken** — orient off the
tower, not the room.

### Group split

![A/B group split](/p2/page04.png)

| Group | Job |
|-------|-----|
| **A** | **Stack + Light-Party buddy.** Takes the stacks on **towers 1–3** (and tower 8) |
| **B** | **Clone & Cone enjoyers** for the first 3 towers… then **B takes over the towers at Tower 4** |

So: **B does the same thing on towers 1–3 and 8; A does the same thing on towers 4–7.** Watch for
the handoff at Tower 4 ("**B take over towers**").

### Left/right priority

Within a tower, who takes the cone/stack on which side is set by our standing priority:

- **Supports** read **left to right** as **RMMR**
- **Stacks** read as **SSDD** (Support, Support, DPS, DPS)
- **Non-tower Supports** always take the **stack / cone on the LEFT**.
- **Non-tower DPS** always stand in the **stack on the RIGHT**.

> Even when *both supports* (or *both DPS*) get the stack, the priority tells you who's right — e.g.
> "both supports got stack, but by prio we know the **T**ank is right"; "both DPS got stack, but by
> prio we know **R**anged is right." The exact per-tower spot is in the diagrams below.

### Odd Towers (1, 3, 5, 7) — Stack · Cone · Spread

![Odd towers — cone & spread](/p2/page07.png)

![Odd towers — stack spacing](/p2/page08.png)

- **Stack** = on the boss **hitbox**, between the outer hitbox and the tower edge. **Not** in the
  corner of the "eye" shape, **not** past the midway point of the tower. (It is *not* the same spot
  as the Even-tower cone.)
  - **Tank** stands **right in front**.
- **Cone** = **away from the stack**, but **not** on the tower's back wall. You are the *driver* of
  this tower — the stack is fixed on the hitbox, so judging whether you're closer to your cone baiter
  or the stack *is* the mechanic. Don't touch the tower edge, sit just off it.
- **Spread** = **"South"** of the tower (away from the boss). If you're opposite the stack player you
  live, so it's whatever.

> Left stack goes **on the boss hitbox ring**; right stack goes **in front**, aligned toward the
> "new north." **Don't go into the corner** — you die to a clone kick. **Don't cross the midpoint** —
> you kill the tank. You have tons of space; open your eyes. *Don't overcook this tower.*

---

### Even Towers (2, 4, 6, 8) — Cone · Spread · Clone bait

![Even towers — Tower 2 rules](/p2/page09.png)

> ⚠️ **EVEN TOWERS CHANGED — pay attention.** This layout gives cone baiters a real waymark to stand
> on. If you progged this phase, even towers are why you were wiping.

- **Cone** = **inside, ON the INNER hitbox ring, at the TOWER EDGE.** Get *way* up in there, where
  the tower crosses the inner ring.
- **Cone Baiter** = **back corner of the number (#) waymarks**, *or* **on the cardinal (letter)
  marker edge toward the boss** (depending on the tower — check the slide).
- **Spread** = **"South"** of the tower, moving **slightly toward the other tower**, opposite of the
  cone.
- **Melee clone baiting = on the OUTER hitbox.**

> **Look at the boss!** Inner hitbox + tower edge = **Cone**. Edge of marker toward boss = **cone
> bait**. South of tower = **Spread**.
>
> ⚠️ **If you are in or on the INNER hitbox ring, you die** (unless you're the cone on the tower
> edge).

**Cone-bait waymark spots** — the baiter stands on the back corner of the number marks, or on the
